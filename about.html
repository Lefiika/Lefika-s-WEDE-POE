Our Journey: From Garage to Digital

Tech Lover’s wasn't built in a corporate office; it was built with screwdrivers and late nights in a small suburban garage. Here is how we grew into the tech provider we are today.

The Tech Lover’s Timeline

2018: The Garage Era

Founders Lefika Polokwane and John Mbusi start a dedicated two-man repair team. Specializing in cracked screens and hardware diagnostic tests, word of mouth travels fast in the local community.

2020: Physical Expansion

Answering the community's call, we move into our first dedicated retail space. We officially add high-quality computer parts to our service list, helping users upgrade their existing machines.

2022: Custom Building

A major milestone as we begin offering both prebuilt and custom-designed computers. We focus on power, reliability, and cooling for gamers and local businesses alike.

2026: The Digital Leap

Now in 2026, we launch our online platform. We bring our expertise directly to your door through regional delivery, making expert tech support accessible nationwide.

Our Mission

The mission of Tech Lover’s is simple: To provide reliable, high-quality tech solutions from expert repairs to custom builds while maintaining the personal, community-focused service that started in our garage. We aim to keep your world connected by making technology accessible, repairable, and built to last.

We believe in honesty above all else. Whether it's a student needing a quick laptop fix or a small business setting up an entire office network, we provide the same level of care and technical precision. We don't just fix computers; we empower our customers to use their technology to its fullest potential without the fear of high costs or technical jargon.

Our Vision for 2030

Our vision is to evolve from a local repair hub into a premier nationwide tech provider by 2030, setting the standard for sustainable technology. We aren't just looking to sell hardware; we are looking to change how people interact with their devices. By integrating a seamless online shopping experience with our deep technical expertise, we ensure that high-quality computer parts and professional IT support are accessible to every home and small business across the country.

We envision a future where "planned obsolescence" is a thing of the past. Our goal is to promote hardware longevity, ensuring that every build we sell is easily upgradeable and every repair we perform extends the life of your tech by years, not months.
