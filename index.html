Our Mission & History

Tech Lover’s was founded in 2018 by Lefika Polokwane and John Mbusi. What began as a humble two-man operation in a small suburban garage has blossomed into one of the most trusted names in local tech support. Our history is rooted in a simple philosophy: quality work and honest pricing. By 2020, we moved into our first retail space, and by 2022, we were shipping custom-built PCs across the region.

Our mission today is to provide reliable, high-quality tech solutions—from the simplest screen repair to the most complex server-grade custom builds. We believe that technology should be accessible and repairable. In an age of "throwaway" electronics, we stand for sustainability and craftsmanship. We want to ensure that every student, gamer, and business owner has access to the tools they need to succeed. Click here to read our full story and see how we plan to change the tech landscape.
